About Me

Hi, I'm Rahul. I'm an editor and that's how I make sense of the world. They say the most personal is the most creative, and I feel that deeply. Music is where most of my days begin and end; you'll usually find me with my headphones on.

I'm drawn to people and to moments that often slip by unnoticed‚Äîthe pauses, the glances, the things left unsaid. I'm fascinated by drama, colours, paintings, history, and different cultures. The list keeps growing, honestly.

I've always wanted to do everything, but one life felt too small. Editing changed that for me. It lets me live many lives at once‚Äîshaping emotion, rhythm, and meaning out of fragments. It's where music, observation, storytelling, and feeling come together. For me, editing isn't just post-production; it's the final act of storytelling.
